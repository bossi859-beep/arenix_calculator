Choose the trader count and entry fee from fixed options. The K40 model is locked in.

K40 rules

Trader count is limited to 10–100 through the fixed selector. Then 40% of traders are paid, 75% of those paid spots are profit positions, 25% are break-even, and everything below the cut line gets $0.

Rank-by-rank payout output

The calculator distributes the 85% payout pool using a top-heavy weighted curve. Every profit rank earns more than the entry fee, break-even positions get the exact entry fee back, and rank 1 is adjusted for rounding so the total matches exactly.
